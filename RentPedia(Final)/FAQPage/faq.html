Here are some frequently asked questions through our website :


  1. What is a boarding house? A boarding house is a type of residential accomodation where guests rent individual rooms and share common areas such as kitchen, living room, and bathroom.

  2. How does the booking process work? To book a boarding house, you can browse the available listings on our website and select the one that meets your needs. You will need to provide your personal information and payment details to confirm your booking.

  3. What types of boarding houses are available? We offer a variety of boarding houses that cater to different needs and preferences, including single rooms and shared rooms.

  4. How do I know if the boarding house is available? You can check the availability of a boarding house, our website provides the services to do so.

  5. Can I cancel my booking? Cancellation policies vary depending on the individual boarding house and are indicated in the listings. If you need to cancel your booking, please contact the property owner or manager directly.
